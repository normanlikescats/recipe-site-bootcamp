Bara Chirashi Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 3 tbsp. rice vinegar
  • 2 tbsp. sugar
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 oz. sashimi-grade salmon
  • 4 oz. sashimi-grade tuna
  • 4 oz. sashimi-grade yellowtail
  • 4 oz. cooked shrimp
  • 4 oz. thinly sliced omelette
  • 4 oz. thinly sliced tamagoyaki (sweet omelette)
  • 1 cup fresh edamame, shelled
  • 2 nori sheets, cut into thin strips
  • Wasabi, soy sauce, and pickled ginger for serving

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the sushi rice in cold water until the water runs clear. Drain and transfer to a medium saucepan.
  2. Add water to the saucepan and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 18-20 minutes, or until all the water is absorbed.
  3. In a small bowl, combine rice vinegar, sugar, and salt. Stir until dissolved.
  4. Transfer the cooked rice to a large mixing bowl and gently fold in the vinegar mixture. Let cool to room temperature.
  5. Beat the eggs in a small bowl and scramble in a non-stick skillet over medium heat until set. Cut into small pieces and set aside.
  6. Cut the sashimi-grade salmon, tuna, and yellowtail into small pieces.
  7. To assemble, place a portion of the sushi rice on a serving plate. Arrange the sashimi, cooked shrimp, omelette, tamagoyaki, scrambled eggs, and edamame on top of the rice.
  8. Sprinkle with nori strips. Serve with wasabi, soy sauce, and pickled ginger on the side. Enjoy!

Ramen Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 servings of fresh ramen noodles
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 tbsp. soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp. miso paste
  • 1 tbsp. sake or rice wine
  • 1 tbsp. mirin
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 4 oz. sliced pork belly or chicken breast
  • 4 oz. sliced shiitake mushrooms
  • 4 oz. sliced green onions
  • 4 soft-boiled eggs, halved
  • 2 sheets nori, cut into thin strips
  • Fresh cilantro and sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the fresh ramen noodles according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pot, bring the broth to a boil. Stir in the soy sauce, miso paste, sake, mirin, garlic, and ginger until combined.
  3. Add the sliced pork belly or chicken breast to the pot and simmer until cooked through.
  4. Add the sliced shiitake mushrooms and green onions to the pot and cook for another 2-3 minutes, or until tender.
  5. To serve, divide the cooked ramen noodles among 4 serving bowls. Ladle the broth and ingredients over the noodles.
  6. Top with the soft-boiled eggs, nori strips, fresh cilantro, and sesame seeds. Enjoy!
