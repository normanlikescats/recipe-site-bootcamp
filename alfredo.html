Alfredo Pasta Recipe

Ingredients: 1 lb. fettuccine pasta 2 cups heavy cream 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese 1/4 cup unsalted butter Salt and pepper to taste Optional toppings: chopped parsley, grated Parmesan cheese Instructions: Cook the fettuccine pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside. In a medium saucepan, heat the heavy cream over medium heat until it begins to simmer. Reduce heat to low and stir in the grated Parmesan cheese and butter until melted and combined. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Toss the cooked fettuccine pasta with the Alfredo sauce until well coated. Serve hot and garnish with chopped parsley and additional grated Parmesan cheese, if desired. Enjoy!
