Ramen Recipe

Ingredients: 4 servings of fresh ramen noodles 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth 2 tbsp. soy sauce 2 tbsp. miso paste 1 tbsp. sake or rice wine 1 tbsp. mirin 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 inch ginger, grated 4 oz. sliced pork belly or chicken breast 4 oz. sliced shiitake mushrooms 4 oz. sliced green onions 4 soft-boiled eggs, halved 2 sheets nori, cut into thin strips Fresh cilantro and sesame seeds for garnish Instructions: Cook the fresh ramen noodles according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside. In a large pot, bring the broth to a boil. Stir in the soy sauce, miso paste, sake, mirin, garlic, and ginger until combined. Add the sliced pork belly or chicken breast to the pot and simmer until cooked through. Add the sliced shiitake mushrooms and green onions to the pot and cook for another 2-3 minutes, or until tender. To serve, divide the cooked ramen noodles among 4 serving bowls. Ladle the broth and ingredients over the noodles. Top with the soft-boiled eggs, nori strips, fresh cilantro, and sesame seeds. Enjoy!
