Alfredo Pasta Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb. fettuccine pasta
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: chopped parsley, grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a medium saucepan, heat the heavy cream over medium heat until it begins to simmer.
  3. Reduce heat to low and stir in the grated Parmesan cheese and butter until melted and combined.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Toss the cooked fettuccine pasta with the Alfredo sauce until well coated.
  6. Serve hot and garnish with chopped parsley and additional grated Parmesan cheese, if desired.


Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 jar of pasta sauce
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Stir in pasta sauce and set aside.
  5. In a separate bowl, beat the egg and mix in ricotta cheese and Parmesan cheese.
  6. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, layer 3 cooked lasagna noodles on the bottom.
  7. Spread a layer of the meat sauce on top of the noodles.
  8. Spread a layer of the cheese mixture on top of the meat sauce.
  9. Repeat the layering process two more times, ending with a layer of mozzarella cheese on top.
  10. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
    11. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  11. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
